Malaysia My Second Home (MM2H)

A long-term visa program that allows foreigners to live in Malaysia comfortably and legally

What is MM2H?

MM2H is a long-term residency visa designed for foreigners who wish to stay in Malaysia for extended periods. It offers multiple benefits including long stay privileges, access to local lifestyle, lower entry price for property purchase/investment and a stable environment for retirement, family living, or long-term relocation.

Why Malaysia & Why Penang?

Malaysia offers a high quality of life with affordable living costs, modern infrastructure, and a warm climate.

Penang is especially popular within expats due to its safety, international schools, excellent healthcare, food culture, and strong expat community.
