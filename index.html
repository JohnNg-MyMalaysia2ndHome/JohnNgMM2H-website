Malaysia My Second Home (MM2H) 马来西亚第二家园

A long-term visa program that allows foreigners to live in Malaysia comfortably and legally 一项允许外国人在马来西亚舒适合法居住的长期签证计划

What is MM2H? 什么是MM2H

MM2H is a long-term residency visa designed for foreigners who wish to stay in Malaysia for extended periods. It offers multiple benefits including long stay privileges, access to local lifestyle, lower entry price for property purchase/investment and a stable environment for retirement, family living, or long-term relocation. MM2H（马来西亚第二家园计划）是一项长期居留签证，专为希望在马来西亚长期居住的外国人设计。 它提供多项福利，包括长期居留特权、体验当地生活方式、较低的购房/投资门槛以及稳定的环境，适合退休、家庭生活或长期移居。

Why Malaysia & Why Penang?

Malaysia offers a high quality of life with AFFORDABLE living costs, modern infrastructure, and a warm climate. 马来西亚拥有高品质的生活，生活成本适中，基础设施现代化，气候温暖宜人。

Penang is especially popular within expats due to its safety, international schools, excellent healthcare, food culture, and strong expat community. 槟城因其安全的环境、国际化的学校、优质的医疗保健、丰富的饮食文化以及强大的外籍人士社区而备受外籍人士的青睐。
