Malaysia My Second Home (MM2H) 马来西亚第二家园

What is MM2H? 什么是MM2H

MM2H is a long-term residency visa designed for foreigners who wish to stay in Malaysia for extended periods. It offers multiple benefits including long stay privileges, access to local lifestyle, lower entry price for property purchase/investment and a stable environment for retirement, family living, or long-term relocation. MM2H（马来西亚第二家园计划）是一项长期居留签证，专为希望在马来西亚长期居住的外国人设计。 它提供多项福利，包括长期居留特权、体验当地生活方式、较低的购房/投资门槛以及稳定的环境，适合退休、家庭生活或长期移居。

Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1:What does the New Application Fee include? 新申请费包含哪些内容？

The Application Fee which is set by the government is inclusive of the agency’s Professional Service Fee, Visa Fee, Participation Fee, Medical Check, Health Insurance, Translation Fee(if applicable) for the Principal. 政府规定的申请费包括代理机构的专业服务费、签证费、参与费、体检费、健康保险费、翻译费（如适用）。

Question 2:How long is the MM2H visa valid for? MM2H签证有效期是多久？

The validity depends on the category approved (such as Silver, Gold, or Platinum), with long-term stay privileges that can be renewed. 有效期取决于获批的签证类别（例如银卡、金卡或白金卡），持有者享有可续签的长期居留权。

Question 3: Can I bring my family under MM2H? 我可以带着家人通过MM2H计划移民吗？

Yes. Spouse, children and parents can be included as dependents under the MM2H program, subject to approval. 是的。配偶、子女和父母可以作为副申请人纳入马来西亚第二家园计划，但需获得批准。

Question 4:How long does the MM2H application process take? 申请MM2H需要多久的时间？

The process will take roughly 3-6 months time till you get approval letter. 从申请到收到批准信，整个过程大约需要3-6个月的时间。

Question 5: Is there a minimum days of stay requirement? 是否有最低居留天数要求？

For applicant less then 50 years old will have minumum of 90 days stay requirement (can be fulfilled & cumulated by dependents), 50 years old and above will be waived from this requirement. 50岁以下的申请人需满足至少90天的停留要求（可由家属代为满足和累积），50岁及以上的申请人可免除此要求。
