What is MM2H? 什么是MM2H

MM2H is a long-term residency visa designed for foreigners who wish to stay in Malaysia for extended periods. It offers multiple benefits including long stay privileges, access to local lifestyle, lower entry price for property purchase/investment, and a stable environment for retirement, family living, or long-term relocation. MM2H（马来西亚第二家园计划）是一项长期居留签证，专为希望在马来西亚长期居住的外国人设计。

Why Malaysia & Why Penang? 为什么选择马来西亚 & 槟城？

Malaysia offers a high quality of life with affordable living costs, modern infrastructure, and a warm climate. 马来西亚拥有高品质的生活，生活成本适中，基础设施现代化，气候温暖宜人。

Penang is especially popular with expats due to its safety, international schools, excellent healthcare, food culture, and strong expat community. 槟城因其安全环境、国际化学校、优质医疗、丰富饮食文化及强大的外籍社区而备受欢迎。

Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: What does the New Application Fee include? 新申请费包含哪些内容？

The Application Fee set by the government includes agency Professional Service Fee, Visa Fee, Participation Fee, Medical Check, Health Insurance, Translation Fee(if applicable). 政府规定的申请费包括代理机构的专业服务费、签证费、参与费、体检费、健康保险费、翻译费（如适用）。

Question 2: How long is the MM2H visa valid for? MM2H签证有效期是多久？

The validity depends on the category approved (Silver, Gold, Platinum), with long-term stay privileges that can be renewed. 有效期取决于获批的签证类别（银卡、金卡或白金卡），持有者享有可续签的长期居留权。

Question 3: Can I bring my family under MM2H? 我可以带着家人通过MM2H计划移民吗？

Yes. Spouse, children and parents can be included as dependents, subject to approval. 是的。配偶、子女和父母可以作为副申请人纳入马来西亚第二家园计划，但需获得批准。

Question 4: How long does the MM2H application process take? 申请MM2H需要多久的时间？

Roughly 3-6 months until approval letter is issued. 整个过程大约需要3-6个月。

Question 5: Is there a minimum stay requirement? 是否有最低居留天数要求？

Applicants under 50 years must stay at least 90 days (can be fulfilled & cumulated by dependents); 50+ years are waived. 50岁以下申请人需满足至少90天的停留要求（可由家属代为满足和累积），50岁及以上申请人可免除。
